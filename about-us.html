A Company Passionate About
Quality of Service

Chemedas is the Chemical procurement, logistics and distribution arm of Edas Global Supply Chain Limited.

We partner with organizations in different industries to ensure that their chemical needs are always satisfied.

We ensure that their operations never stops as a result of lack of raw materials or chemicals because we have devised ways by which we work with them on their monthly, bi-monthly, quarterly or annual MRP (Material Requirement Planning), and ensure that their needs are adequately met.

This is why we have trusts from our clients in the Oil and Gas sector, Food industries, health, cosmetics and construction.

Positioned to meet your requirements, we have our Customer Services & Sales Teams in place to support your business needs. We offer full support from our base in Lagos, as well as area Sales support from our team operating across the country having a local Sensitivity with a Global Perspective.

We are aspiring to be the most preferred chemical procurement and logistics partner globally and we have a mission to ensure that at every point in time, our clients are never out of stock for materials they need to operate successfully.

Quality Service delivery, Integrity and Compliance remains our core values.
